About Vibe Real Estate

A new and contemporary brokerage in the Northern Emirates, founded by award-winning agents committed to excellence, transparency, and results.

Our Mission

Friendly. Professional. Transparent.

Vibe Real Estate is a contemporary brokerage located in the Northern Emirates, founded by award-winning agents. We solely operate in the Northern Emirates, catering to clients both locally and internationally — helping them find the right property for investors, end users and holiday makers.

We specialise in high-potential real estate investments, with a focus on land deals and premium properties in Ras Al Khaimah and Dubai. Our team helps investors and developers access off-market opportunities and navigate the UAE market with confidence.
